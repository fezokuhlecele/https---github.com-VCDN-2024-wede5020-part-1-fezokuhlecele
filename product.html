Our Products & Services

At SeniorTech, we offer a range of products and services designed to support seniors on their journey to digital literacy. From training programs to specialized tech support, we ensure our solutions are accessible, affordable, and easy to use.

Basic Digital Literacy Training

Our beginner-friendly courses help seniors learn essential digital skills, including how to use smartphones, tablets, and computers. These interactive lessons are designed for absolute beginners.

Online Safety and Security Workshop

This workshop teaches seniors how to stay safe online. It covers topics such as identifying phishing scams, creating secure passwords, and maintaining privacy on social media.

Tech Support for Seniors

Our personalized tech support services assist seniors in troubleshooting technical issues, setting up devices, and understanding new software. We offer both remote and in-home support options.

Senior-Friendly Devices

We provide devices that are specifically tailored for seniors, featuring large screens, simplified interfaces, and easy-to-use features. These devices are perfect for seniors looking to stay connected without complexity.

Community Learning Groups

We host local and online group sessions where seniors can learn together, share experiences, and receive guidance from peers and trainers in a supportive environment.
